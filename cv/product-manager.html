    What I thought of managing digital products

    For me, properly delivering information is the core business to so-called Digital Products.

    Gather, extract, compose then deliver are long process that majoritiy of businesses need to pass through nowadays. At the very end, necessary steps are taken to make it available on phone, tablets and web, to go along with diversed digital consumers.

    First, in consideration of media, culture, language, level of audiences, and complexity of the content itself, they need to have proper UI/UX designs.

    Then, to effectively manage data circulation and transformation, accross applications and databases, adaption of new technologies are required, with the hope of either lowering down operational costs or having more stable application.

    Lastly, in business perspective, they also need to analyze their product update to measure the business impact, whether it is positive or negative.

    Therefore, these are what Product Manager required to do in his daily activity, ensuring team‑work of 3 separated teams. He must helps good coordination and suggests measureable actions to grow the business. Furthermore, to justify the effort will be matched by conversions for its targeted audience, one may also utilizes User Paths and Funnels.
